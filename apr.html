Saint Tyagaraja

The Worship of Music & Bharat

The Spirit of Nadopasana

Saint Tyagaraja believed that music wasn't just a skillâ€”it was the soul of Bharat. He called it Nadopasana: a way to reach the Divine through every note. He spent his life exploring the Rasa (essence) of every Raga, proving that music is the highest form of service to humanity.

He lived a simple life of Unchavritti, walking the streets and singing for his daily bread. He famously turned down mountains of gold from kings because he believed his talent belonged to God alone.
