Guru Nanak Dev Ji

"Truth is the highest virtue, but higher still is truthful living."

Universal Teachings

Guru Nanak Dev Ji (1469–1539), the founder of Sikhism, taught a message of Ek Onkar—the oneness of the Divine. He stood against caste hierarchies and gender inequality, establishing the tradition of Langar (free community kitchen) to prove that all humans are equal in the eyes of the Creator.

The Mool Mantar

"God is One. He is the Supreme Truth. He is the Creator. He is without fear and without hate. He is immortal, beyond birth and death. He is self-illumined."
