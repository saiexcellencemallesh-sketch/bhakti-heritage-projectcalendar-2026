Tulsidas: The Evolution

A mortal passion transformed into a divine flame.

The Severe Storm

Unable to stay away from Ratnavali, Tulsidas faced a thunderstorm so fierce the world seemed to end. The rain blinded the eyes, but his desire blinded his soul.

Blinded by Attachment

He reached the river Yamuna, flooded and dangerous. In his desperation to cross, he grabbed a human corpse thinking it was a log.

The Mirror of Truth

After climbing a venomous snake to reach her, his wife delivered the rebuke that changed history: "If you loved God half as much..."

The Shift

The illusion collapsed. He walked away into the light of Truth. A saint was born from the ruins of desire.
