The Divine Incarnation

Andal (8th Century) is the only female among the twelve Alvar saints of South India. Found as a baby under a Tulsi plant, she is celebrated as 'Chudikudutha Sudarkodi'—the woman who gave the Lord the garland she had first worn herself.
