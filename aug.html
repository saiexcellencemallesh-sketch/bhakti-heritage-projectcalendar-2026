Sharada Maa

August 2026

The Mother of All

Sharada Maa, the Holy Mother, was the spiritual partner of Sri Ramakrishna. While she lived a very quiet life, she embodied a type of love that the world had rarely seen—unconditional, silent, and incredibly strong.

Her Compassion: She didn't just love the good people; she loved everyone exactly the same. She famously said that she was the mother of the wicked just as much as she was the mother of the virtuous. To her, no one was a stranger. Her life was a silent prayer for the welfare of all her children across the entire world. She taught us that the greatest power in the universe isn't money or fame, but a simple, motherly heart that accepts everyone without judgment.
