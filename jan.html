What is Bhakthi?

Bhakthi is just a simple, pure love. It is the special bond between your heart and the Divine. It isn’t about hard rules or difficult rituals. It is more like a "romance of the soul"—a way to feel connected to something bigger than yourself just by being who you are. When you sing with joy or remember a name with love, you are in the flow of Bhakthi.

The Soul of Bharath

This love is the heartbeat of Bharath. The word actually means "those who are engaged in light"—where Bha means the light of wisdom. For thousands of years, our land has been held together by a "sacred geography". The saints didn't just pray; they walked and sang across this land, making it holy with every step. From the high mountains to the deep oceans, this thread of devotion is what truly defines our home.

"It is this thread of Bhakthi that has defined the sacred geography of Bharath."
