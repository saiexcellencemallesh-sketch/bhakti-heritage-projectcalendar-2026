Sacred Vibrations

The Value of the One

Sadguru Sri Madhusudan Sai emphasises to the students that when bhakti (devotion to God) is there before anything else, everything in life will be taken care of and will gain more and more value. He simplifies this concept with a beautiful analogy anyone can understand: Behind the number 1, any amount of zeroes will only add to its value. But when we remove that 1; no matter how many zeroes you add, the value will still be zero.

The Intensity of Emotion

Bhakti Yoga means using the intensity of one's emotion to transcend one's limitations. Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev explains how being a devotee is the most beautiful way to exist.
